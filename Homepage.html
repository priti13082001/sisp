Student Information System for Placement

Automation

This is one of the major problems which the placement team, face in our campus. We are trying to solve this issue by creating a separate portal for placement team so that they can switch over from the manually operated excel sheets to fast and efficient database management with quick and easy access to the student database.

Templates

One of the most amazing feature of our product is to generate and store views of data based on the templates provided by the companies, which reduces redundant selections and filtering. Also, respects the integrity of the database and makes it error-free.

Future Prospects

We at SISP are trying to create a platform where all the work of the placement team can be managed efficiently. After successfully implementing basic components, we plan on integrating this portal further, such as creating a summary sheet of students based on companies etc.
